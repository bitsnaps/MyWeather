Help for My Weather

Adding a new city

From the menu, tap Add City. In the new screen that appears, pan and zoom the map to where you want it. Tap the map to drop a pin. Enter a name for this city. Tap Save to confirm your new city, or tap the back button to cancel.

Viewing today's forecast for a city

Tap the name of the city in the list and a forecast screen will appear.

Refreshing the forecasts

The latest weather forecasts will automatically be downloaded when the app starts, and when the device is rotated.

Deletng a city

From the city list, tap the X button next to the city you want to delete. It will no longer appear in the list.

Settings

From the menu, tap Settings to see the settings screen.

Deleting all cities

On the settings screen, tap the Clear All button to remove all cities.
